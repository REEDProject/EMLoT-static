Notes
The 'old poet' is the anonymous writer of the 'On the Time-Poets,' the poem where the lines this record is based upon come from. It is first published in R.P., 'Choyce Drollery,' London, 1656. The lines in question (about John Ford) appear on page 6. The lines are picked up again in Gerard Langbaine, 'An Account of the English Dramatick Poets,' London, 1691. (Both 'Choyce' and 'Account' are available on EEBO.) On page 219 of this document, the lines are introduced as being written by an 'old man.' The later 'Select Collection of Old Plays' from which this record was originally generated uses Langbaine as its source.
