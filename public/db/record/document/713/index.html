Title

Full title
A Cast Over the Water, by John Taylor. Given Gratis to William Fennor, the Rimer, from London to the Kings Bench. Or a Replication to Fennors answer. With Admonitions, and friendly Exhortations in Prose and Verse, perswading the said Fennor to Penitence, that he may hang with the clearer conscience at St Thomas of Waterings. Heere you may see a fellow brau'd and baffled,/ And (like a Iade) is spurgal'd, swicht, and snaffled.
