Viewing Document Record: Theses Martinianae

Citation

Primary author(s)
Marprelate, Martin
Title
Theses Martinianae
Full title
Theses Martinianae: That is, Certaine Demonstrative Conclusions, sette downe and collected (as it should seeme) by that famous and renowmed Clarke, the reverend Martin Marprelate the great: serving as a manifest and sufficient confutation of al that ever the Colledge of Catercaps with their whole band of Clergie-priests, have, or can bring for the defence of their ambitious and Antichristian Prelacie. Published and set foorthe as an after-birth of the noble Gentleman himselfe, by a prety stripling of his, Martin Junior, and dedicated by him to his good neame and nuncka, Maister John Kankerbury: How the yong man came by them, the Reader shall understande sufficiently in the Epilogue. In the meane time, whosoeuer can bring mee acquainted with my father, Ile bee bounde hee shall not loose his labour
