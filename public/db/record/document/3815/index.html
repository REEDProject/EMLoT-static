Citation

Primary author(s)

Title
Taylor's Revenge
Full title
Taylors Revenge or The Rymer Willima Fennor Firkt, Feritted, and finely fetcht ouer the Coales. Wherein His Riming Raggamuffin Rascallity without Partiallity, or feare or Principallity, is Anagramatized, Anotomized, & Stigmatized. The occasion of which Inuectiue, is breifly set downe in the Preface to the Reader. Reuenge doth Gallop when it seemes to creepe, / For though my wrong did winke, it did not sleepe.
