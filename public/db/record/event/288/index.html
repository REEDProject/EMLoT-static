Viewing Event Record: The post-mortem inquisition of Thomas Brend names Shakespeare in occupation of property in St Saviour's

Abstract

The inquisition post-mortem for Thomas Brend, father to Nicholas, dated 16 May 1599, refers to a newly built playhouse in the parish of St Saviour, Surrey, 'in the occupation of William Shakespeare and others.'
