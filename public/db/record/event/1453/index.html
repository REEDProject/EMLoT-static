Viewing Event Record: The King's Men and Prince Charles' Men are allowed cloth to wear in King James' funeral procession

Abstract

The King's Men and Prince Charles' Men, as well as Master of Revels Henry Herbert and the staff of the Revels office are allowed cloth for outfits to wear in King James' funeral procession.
