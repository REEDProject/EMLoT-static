Viewing Event Record: Chancery, Jane Langley vs Hugh Browker: Thomas Jadwin responds

Abstract

In a Chancery suit between Jane Langley and Hugh Browker regarding the sale of Paris Garden, scrivener Thomas Jadwin deposes that Francis Langley borrowed 100 pounds from Nicholas Satterley, with Browker as surety, and that Browker eventually repaid the debt.
