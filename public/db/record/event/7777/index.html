Viewing Event Record: Robert Shaw sells 'The Four Sons of Aymon' to Henslowe and the Admiral's Men

Abstract

In the memorandum book of Philip Henslowe, player Robert Shaw records receipt of payment in the amount of forty shillings for a play entitled 'The Four Sons of Aymon,' which if not actively played by the following Christmas would be returned with refund.
