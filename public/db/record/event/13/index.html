Viewing Event Record: The Globe burns: Sir Henry Wotton

Abstract

Sir Henry Wotton writes to Sir Edmund Bacon with a detailed and minutely descriptive account of the burning of the Globe theatre, which caught fire during the masquing scene at the performance of a new King's Men play called 'All Is True.'
