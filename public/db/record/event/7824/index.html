Viewing Event Record: William Spence sells Rose tenements to Henry Polsted

Abstract

A 1537 deed describes the sale of Bankside properties by William Spence to Henry Polsted, including 'his Tenement and gardens sometyme called The Rose' as well as 'the Barge'.
