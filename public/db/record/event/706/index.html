Viewing Event Record: Passages in "Tarltons Jests" refer to incidents at the Bull

Abstract

Passages in "Tarltons Jests" refer to incidents at the Bull: in the first instance, an audience member in the gallery threw a pippin at Tarlton when he came onto the stage; in the second, Tarlton took the place of an absent cast member who was to play the judge in Henry the Fifth, in addition to 'his own part of the clown'.
