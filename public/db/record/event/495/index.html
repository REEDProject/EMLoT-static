Viewing Event Record: The lord mayor of London asks Walsingham to forbid plays at playhouses in the liberties because of plague

Abstract

The lord mayor writes to Sir Francis Walsingham that theatres and bear-baiting rings -- the Theatre and Curtain specifically -- are a great threat to spread of plague. He asks Walsingham to convince the privy council to close the places outside the Mayor's jurisdiction.
