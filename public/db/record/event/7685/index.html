Viewing Event Record: Earl of Bedford requests the return of mastiffs from Edward Alleyn

Abstract

In a letter to Edward Alleyn, the Earl of Bedford requests the return of a mastiff dog, taken from a servant named Edward Parkines by officers sent into the country 'for the takinge of certaine mastife dogges in his maiesties name for Parrish garden'.
