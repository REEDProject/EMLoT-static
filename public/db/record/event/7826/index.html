Viewing Event Record: Holinshed describes the collapse of Beargarden scaffolds

Abstract

Raphael Holinshed reports that in 'An. Reg. 25' of Queen Elizabeth [1583] the 'old and underpropped' scaffolds at the Beargarden 'fell suddenlie downe,' killing eight people and 'manie other sore hurt.'

Date notes

Notes
Holinshed dates the Chronicle with regnal years, and the Beargarden collapse occurs in 'An. Reg. 25' of the 'Queene Elisabeth' section.
