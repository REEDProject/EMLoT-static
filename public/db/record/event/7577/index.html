Viewing Event Record: Thomas Burnaby leases a tenement and Bear Garden property to Richard Reve

Abstract

Upon the death of its tenant, Thomas Burnaby leases a tenement connected to the Bear Garden to Richard Reve, along with the Bear Garden itself and all properties, animals, and associated profits from the use of the baiting grounds. The term of the lease is seven years at a rent of 120 pounds per year.
