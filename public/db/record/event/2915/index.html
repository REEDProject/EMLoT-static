Viewing Event Record: William Percy's Arabia Sitiens offers notes for performance at St Paul's

Abstract

A note on properties for William Percy's Arabia Sitiens offers alternate production advice for 'Actors' and 'Powles.' If the play is performed by Actors, he suggests 'A Huge Globe of Fyre Pendaunt from the Roofe of the House'; if for the Children of Paul's, 'A Full onely appearing on face of the stage thourough the cloth and right over the Tribunall.'
