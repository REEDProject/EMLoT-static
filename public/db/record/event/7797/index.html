Viewing Event Record: Samuel Rowley urges Philip Henslowe to pay authors of 'The Six Yeomen of the West'

Abstract

In a letter, playwright Samuel Rowley urges Philip Henslowe to pay playwrights John Day and William Haughton the remainder of their fee for 'the syx yemen of the weste.' On the reverse of the letter, Day writes an excerpt of verse and requests that his share be paid to 'Will Hunt, the Pedler.'
