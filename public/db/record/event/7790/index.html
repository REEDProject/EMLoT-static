Viewing Event Record: Edward Alleyn lists costs for the building of the Fortune

Abstract

In his memorandum book, Edward Alleyn records a general list of costs related to the building of the Fortune theatre, including the acquisition of the land leases and the construction of the playhouse and other buildings. He lists total cost 'in all' as 1320 pounds.
