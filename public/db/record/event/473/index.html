Viewing Event Record: King's, Queen's and Prince's companies are allowed to perform at the Globe, Fortune and Curtain after Lent

Abstract

The privy council issues an order to the Lord Mayor and justices of Surrey and Middlesex to allow the King's, Queen's and Prince's companies to perform at the Globe, Fortune and Curtain.
