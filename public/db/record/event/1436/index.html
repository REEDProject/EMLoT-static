Viewing Event Record: A prospective order from the Privy Council is to prohibit all manner of public entertainment in London, Middlesex and Surrey

Abstract

A 'List of Orders to be published by the Lord Mayor and Aldermen of the City of London and the Justices of the Peace of the Counties of Middlesex and Surrey by direction from the Lords of the Privy Council' is to forbid 'playes Bearebaytinges games singing of balletts buckler play or such like causes of assemblies,' under pain of severe punishment.
