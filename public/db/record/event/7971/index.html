Viewing Event Record: Jonson's 'The Staple of News' makes reference to 'A Game at Chess' controversy

Abstract

In Ben Jonson's comedy 'The Staple of News,' the character Thomas alludes to a 'Legacy left to the King's Players' and to the staging of Gondomar's likeness, with marginal annotations clarifying the reference to 'the game at Chesse, or Play so called.'
