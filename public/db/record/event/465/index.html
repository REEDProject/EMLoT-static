Viewing Event Record: Alleyn tallies his expenditures for the Fortune and Bear Garden

Abstract

Edward Alleyn lists in a summary table money spent on the Fortune and Bear Garden between 1602 and 1608. Alleyn spent £845 8s 11d on the Fortune and £846 4s 10d on the Bear Garden. The reverse side of the document contains an unexplained table of accounts totaling £1191 2s 4d followed by the statement, 'Besids ye money wch wase / Taken vp for ye Reulls.'
