Viewing Event Record: Chancery, John Henslowe vs Edward Alleyn et al: Alleyn must submit to the court all documents relating to the John Henslowe suit

Abstract

Chancery Court orders Edward Alleyn to return to court all evidences and writings regarding the John Henslowe [deceased] case currently in his possession, as William Henslowe, William and Ann Parsons, and Mary Addington claim a right to the documents, according to the terms set out in Phillip Henslowe's will.
