Viewing Event Record: Chamberlain's Men perform Jonson's 'Every Man in his Humour': cast list

Abstract

The 1616 folio edition of Ben Jonson's 'Works' contains a list of principle cast members of the Chamberlain's Men's 1598 first production of 'Every Man in his Humour.' Following the cast list is a notice that the play was performed 'With the allowance of the Master of Revels.'
