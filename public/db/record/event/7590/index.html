Viewing Event Record: Richard Cuckow assumes the lease on the six parcels of the Newington Butts property, excepting the playhouse site

Abstract

Following the death of Richard Hickes, Richard Cuckowe assumes the lease on the six parcels of land comprising the Newington Butts property. The lease excepts a segment of the piece known as Lurklane, the tenement which Hickes erected the playhouse, now enclosed and occupied by Peter Honingborne.
