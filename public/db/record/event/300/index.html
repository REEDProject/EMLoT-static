Viewing Event Record: William Longe sells the Curtain estate to Thomas Harberte

Abstract

The Curtain estate changes hands again: William Longe, son of Maurice Longe, sells the property to Thomas Harberte. The contract describes the estate in detail similar to that of 20 February 1567.
