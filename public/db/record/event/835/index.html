Viewing Event Record: George Ferrers, Gentleman of Lincoln's Inn, takes the office of Lord of Misrule at Christmas

Abstract

Stowe's Annals record that George Ferrers, Gentleman of Lincoln's Inn, took the office of Lord of Misrule at the Court at Greenwich over the Christmas season, and that the 'K. had great delight in his pastimes.'
