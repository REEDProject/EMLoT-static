Viewing Event Record: The privy council orders a stay on plans to erect a new playhouse in the Whitefriars precinct

Abstract

In light of complaints from the Recorder and Aldermen of London, the privy council orders a stay on plans to erect a new playhouse in the Whitefriars precinct.
