Viewing Event Record: St Paul's is defaced

Abstract

Stow's 'Survey of London' (1598) describes the southern exterior of St Paul's -- which may have included the approach to the playhouse -- as defaced 'by meanes of Lycences graunted to Cutlers, Budget makers, and other, first to build lowe sheddes, but now higher Houses, which do hide that beautifull side of the Churche.'
