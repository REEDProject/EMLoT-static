Viewing Event Record: Francis Henslowe leases property from Francis Langley at Paris Garden

Abstract

Token books from St. Saviour's church record the new arrival of 'Mr Francis Henslowe' and his household to Francis Langley's Paris Garden tenements in 1595, and their eventual departure from the parish by 1598-9.
