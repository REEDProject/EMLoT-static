Viewing Event Record: A recent fencing match at the Swan ends badly: John Chamberlain [letter]

Abstract

John Chamberlain writes to Dudley Carleton of a recent match at the Swan between two fencers named Dun and Turner. Turner ran Dun through the eye with a foil, killing him instantly.
