Viewing Event Record: Henslowe records performance receipts from the Rose

Abstract

In his memorandum book, Philip Henslowe records his share of the takings from play performances by the Admiral's Men at the Rose playhouse. Plays include 'tasso', 'the doctor fostes', and 'tamberlen'.
