Viewing Event Record: Queen Anne's Men perform 'The Travels of the Three English Brothers'

Abstract

The 1607 title-page of 'The Travels of Three English Brothers' claims it is 'now played by her Maiesties Seruants.'

Date Event Recorded

Date
From: 1607 (Source of claim: original)

Date Event Happened

Date
From: before 29 June 1607 (Source of claim: externally supplied)

Notes on associations

Notes
The Stationers' Register entry names the play 'as yt Was played at the Curten.' (See Database of Early English Drama.)
