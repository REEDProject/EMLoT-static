Viewing Event Record: Several players are sworn in as Prince Charles' Men

Abstract

A warrant is recorded by the Lord Chamberlain to swear in several players as grooms of the chamber in ordinary without fee, so that they may serve as Prince Charles' Men. The final name in the list, George Stutville, was added in after the other names had been written.
