Viewing Event Record: Alleyn purchases the freehold of the Fortune property

Abstract

With the help of Patrick Brewe, Edward Alleyn purchases the freehold of the Fortune property from its owners, Daniel Gill the elder and his two sons, four daughters and their husbands, for Â£340. The property includes twelve tenements and the Fortune playhouse. The contract describes the situation of the tenements, playhouse, and premises, and names the adjoining property owners.
