Viewing Event Record: Chancery, Thomas Woodford vs Aaron Holland: court dismisses the suit

Abstract

The court dismisses Thomas Woodford's suit against Aaron Holland and orders Woodford to pay Holland £2 6s 8d 'for want of a Replication.' The court declares that the matter of Woodford's complaints are 'cleerlie and absolutelie dismissed out of the courte.'
