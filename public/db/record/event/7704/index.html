Viewing Event Record: Edward Alleyn 'rec. rent att ye banksid and Fortune'

Abstract

In a memorandum book entry, Edward Alleyn records that he 'rec. rent att ye banksid and Fortune', and some days later 'dind wt ye company att ye Fortune'.
