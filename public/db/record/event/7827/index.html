Viewing Event Record: Bishop of Winchester demises Barge, Bell, and Cock properties to William Payne

Abstract

A deed records that the Bishop of Winchester demises 'certen capytall messes and tenements called the barge, the bell and the cocke.' The properties sit between a number of other tenements and landmarks, including 'the rose' and 'Mayden lane.'
