Viewing Event Record: A signet bill confirms Phillip Rosseter's patent for the new Porter's Hall playhouse

Abstract

A signet bill dated 31 May 1615 approves Philip Rosseter's patent for the projected playhouse at Porter's Hall. The details of the bill duplicate those laid out in the patent dated 3 June 1615.
