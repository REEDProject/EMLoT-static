Viewing Event Record: Players are liveried servants of the Queen and therefore superior to the Martin Marprelate writers

Abstract

The address to the reader of 'Martin's Month's Mind' reminds the reader that 'Martin' and his sons called the players rogues, though 'indeede they are hir Maiesties men' and the players are more successful servants to the Queen than the Martinist writers.
