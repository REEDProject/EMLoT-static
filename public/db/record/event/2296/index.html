Viewing Event Record: W. R. writes an epitaph for Thomas Greene

Abstract

The 1614 quarto of 'Greene's Tu Quoque' contains a couplet 'vpon the death of Thomas Greene' signed by 'W. R.' (perhaps Williams Rowley). It reads: 'How fast bleake Autumne changeth Floraes dye, / What yesterday was (Greene) now's seare & dry.'
