Viewing Event Record: Dorington to Henslowe: Illness prevents him from mounting an exhibition for the Queen; Henslowe is to step in for him

Abstract

John Dorington, Master of the Queen's Game, writes to Philip Henslowe. The Queen has suddenly requested an exhibition of game at court, and Dorington is too ill to comply with the request. Henslowe is to step in for him.
