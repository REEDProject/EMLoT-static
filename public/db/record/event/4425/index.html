Viewing Event Record: Brome's 'The Antipodes' makes reference to a prompter

Abstract

During a play-within-a-play in Richard Brome's 'The Antipodes' a voice is heard from 'Within' and Letoy onstage says, 'cannot you heare the prompter? / Ha' you lost your eares, Judge?'
