Viewing Event Record: A purse is stolen at the Curtain

Abstract

William Haukins, a barber of Middlesex, is charged with having stolen a purse containing 26s 6d during a performance at the Curtain. Nicholas Collyns, justice of the peace, takes a bond from Richard Fletcher of ten pounds so that Fletcher will appear to testify against Haukins.
