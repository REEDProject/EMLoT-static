Viewing Event Record: Gilbert Katharens and John Browne plan for the construction of the Hope theatre

Abstract

In a series of articles of agreement, builder Gilbert Katharens hires John Browne, a bricklayer, to perform masonry work for 'one Game place or plaie house, a bull howse and a stable' on the location of the old Beargarden. The structure is cited to be 'of as large a compasse and height as the plaie howse called the Swan in the libertie of Parris Garden'.
