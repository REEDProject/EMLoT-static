Viewing Event Record: St Saviour's drafts a petition to the Privy Council to have the local playhouses put down

Abstract

The vestry of St Saviour's resolves to draft a petition to the Privy Council, outlining the 'enormities' that the local playhouses bring to the parish, and requesting that they be put down.
