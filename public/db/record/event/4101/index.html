Viewing Event Record: The Theatre and Curtain destroy the body and soul: Field

Abstract

In his pamphlet about the partial collapse of Paris Garden during a Sunday bearbaiting, John Field observes that 'Heathenish Enterludes and Plaies' performed at 'the Theater, the Curtin and such like' lead to the 'distruction both of bodye and soule.'
