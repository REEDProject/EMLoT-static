Viewing Event Record: Henslowe records loans to Admiral's Men

Abstract

In his memorandum book, Philip Henslowe records loans to the Admiral's Men for various purposes, including the purchase of stage properties such as a 'bvgell dvblett' and payment advances for the composition of playbooks by dramatists such as George Chapman and Henry Chettle.
