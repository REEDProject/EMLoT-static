Viewing Event Record: Queen Anne's Men are given black livery cloth for her funeral procession

Abstract

The players of Queen Anne's Men are allowed four yards of black cloth each for mourning liveries for her funeral procession of 13 May 1619. Samuel Daniel and Robert Killigrew are given nine yards as Grooms of the Queen's Privy Chamber, plus four yards for their servants.
