Viewing Event Record: The Children of Paul's and the Children of the Chapel performed Alexander and Campaspe on Twelfth Night

Abstract

The 1584 edition of Lyly's Alexander and Campaspe advertises that the play was performed by the Children of Paul's and the Children of the Chapel on Twelfth Night, before the Queen. The 1584 edition is extant in two version, STC: 17047.5 and STC: 17048
