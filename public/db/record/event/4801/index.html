Viewing Event Record: The Chamberlain's Men perform at court

Abstract

A warrant for Â£20 provides evidence the Lord Chamberlain's Men played at Whitehall on Holy Innocents' Day, 1594. William Kemp, William Shakespeare and Richard Burbage accept the payment on the companies behalf for this and one further performance.

Date notes

Notes
Chambers (ES, vol 4, p 56) argues that Shakespeare's 'Comedy of Errors' was performed by the Chamberlain's Men for the Gentlemen of the Inner Temple, 28 December 1594. The Treasurer of the Chamber's record of a performance by the company at Whitehall on the same night must therefore be a "slip." Chambers suggests instead that the Chamberlain's Men were at court along with the Admiral's Men on the 27th.
