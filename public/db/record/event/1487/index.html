Viewing Event Record: Theatres close because of the plague

Abstract

On 17 April 1630, the Lord Chamberlain's office issues a warrant to Sir Henry Herbert to suppress all playhouses and other assemblies for sport, due to the increase of plague in London, along with a warrant to Gabriel Marsh to suppress bear and bull-baiting.
