Viewing Event Record: The Lord Chamberlain requests that his company be allowed to play at the Cross Keys

Abstract

A letter from the Lord Hunsdon, the Lord Chamberlain, to the Lord Mayor, requesting that his company be allowed to play a the Cross Keys this winter, as has been their custom in the past. He assures the Lord Mayor that they will conclude their performances between 4 and 5, refrain from using drums or trumpets for calling the people together, and that they will contribute to the support of the parish poor.
