Viewing Event Record: Henslowe pays bond to Tilney for maintenance of order at the Rose

Abstract

Philip Henslowe makes a record in his notebook indicating the completion of payment of a bond of 'one hundreth powndes' to Edmund Tilney, Master of the Revels, to insure the Rose against the disruption of law and order.
