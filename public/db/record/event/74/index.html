Viewing Event Record: Star Chamber, 'Widow Waking' case: Thomas Dekker deposes a second time

Abstract

Thomas Dekker deposes that he, John Webster, William Rowley and John Ford were "privy, consenting and acquainted" with the composition of "Keep the Widow Waking," and that they wrote the play on the instructions of Ralph Savage. Dekker has seen all or part of the play performed several times; he cannot say how often.
