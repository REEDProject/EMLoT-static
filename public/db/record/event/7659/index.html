Viewing Event Record: The Surrey and Kent Commissioners of the Sewers summon Francis Langley for having failed to repair the mill bridge

Abstract

The Surrey and Kent Commissioners for the Sewers summon Francis Langley to explain his failure to repair the mill bridge (as by the order of 5 October), to offer a reason why he should not pay the 20l penalty, and, finally, to stand to the order of the court.
