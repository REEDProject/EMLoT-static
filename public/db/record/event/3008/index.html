Viewing Event Record: Kemp publishes an account of his morris dance from London to Norwich

Abstract

William Kemp danced a morris from London to Norwich in February 1600. He gives a complete account of the journey in his pamphlet.

Date notes

Notes
Date of Kemp's beginning his morris taken from DNB. Kemp reports he began his journey 'the first Munday in cleane Lent'.
