Viewing Event Record: Robert Fludd's 'De naturali' includes an illustration of the 'Globe theatre'

Abstract

As part of his 1619 treatise 'De Naturali, Supernaturali, Praeternaturali, et Contranaturali Microcosmi Historia,' Robert Fludd includes an illustration of a theatre, as an aid to the art of memory. The stage has a title plaque that reads "THEATRUM ORBI,' or 'Globe Theatre,' as well as several doorways and balcony entrances.
