Viewing Event Record: Chancery, Brayne v Burbage: The court rejects Hone's report, continues contempt investigation of the Burbages

Abstract

Margaret Brayne sues James and Cuthbert Burbage in Chancery court over her share of the Theatre playhouse profits. Sarjeant Harris, counsel for the Burbages, rejects the finding that the Burbages should be arrested for contempt. The court agrees with Harris and orders Drs Edward Stanhope and Thomas Legg to report further.
