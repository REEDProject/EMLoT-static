Viewing Event Record: Vestry of St. Saviour's sets Paris Garden tithes

Abstract

At meeting of the churchwardens of St. Saviour's parish, the vestry offers Francis Langley a rate of four pounds yearly 'of all his tithes' in Paris Garden. Langley refuses and offers the lesser sum of 'but four marks a year'. In the years following, the vestry endeavours to review 'Mr Langley's new buildings for the rating of tithes accordingly'.
