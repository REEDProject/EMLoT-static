Project Information

Team and Sponsors

Early Modern London Theatres (EMLoT) could not have been created without the help of the following institutions and individuals, whose contributions are gratefully acknowledged:

Financial Sponsors

  • The Arts and Humanities Research Council (UK) (AHRC) was the principal sponsor for creating the database as a freely accessible web resource.
  • The British Academy sponsored editorial work in Canada to prepare the ‘London Theatres Bibliography’ records for the EMLoT database and has continued to provide essential funding for ongoing bibliographic and editorial work on versions 1, 2, and 3 records.
  • The Social Sciences and Humanities Research Council of Canada (SSHRC) sponsored the creation of the Learning Zone and bibliographic research and editorial support for versions 1 and 2 records.
  • Records of Early English Drama (REED) sponsored all aspects of the project through editorial staff, consultancy, management, and physical and academic resources.
  • King’s College London co-sponsored the creation of the database as a freely accessible web resource through its Centre for Computing in the Humanities (CCH).
  • The University of Southampton co-sponsored the creation of the database as a freely accessible web resource through its Faculty of Humanities.
