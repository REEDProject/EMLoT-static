How to Cite EMLoT

Following MLA conventions:

Records of Early English Drama (REED). Early Modern London Theatres (EMLoT). Version 1 (February 2011). Web. [Record ID or Search Term used]. Accessed [date].

 

Following MHRA conventions:

footnote:

[Record ID or Search Term], in Early Modern London Theatres (EMLoT), version 1 (February 2011), [date accessed]. 

bibliography:

Early Modern London Theatres (EMLoT), version 1 (February 2011).

 

The full definition of the database in its current version is as follows:

Early Modern London Theatres (EMLoT), version 1 (February 2011): Records pertaining to the Eight Theatres north of the Thames [Red Lion (1567), the Theatre (1576), the Curtain (1577), the Fortune (1600), the Red Bull (1604), the Boar's Head (1602), the Phoenix or Cockpit (1616), and Salisbury Court (1629)].

 

The next version of the database will incorporate the Bankside theatres in the historic county of Surrey.
