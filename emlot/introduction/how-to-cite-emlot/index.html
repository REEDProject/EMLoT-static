How to Cite EMLoT

Following MLA conventions:

Records of Early English Drama (REED). Early Modern London Theatres (EMLoT). Web. [Record ID or Search Term used]. Accessed [date].


Following MHRA conventions:

footnote:
[Record ID or Search Term], in Early Modern London Theatres (EMLoT) (April 2024) [date accessed].

bibliography:
Early Modern London Theatres (EMLoT) (April 2024).
