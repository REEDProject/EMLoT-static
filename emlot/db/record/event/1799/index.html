Viewing Event Record: The privy council orders theatres closed because of the plague

Abstract

The Privy Council orders a complete ban on all forms of public spectacle in London and the counties of Middlesex and Surrey while the plague is on the rise. N. B. The document is also abstracted in the Privy Council Register.
