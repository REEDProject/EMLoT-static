Viewing Event Record: Prince Henry's Men are granted livery for Prince Henry's funeral

Abstract

A book from the Lord Chamberlain's office records that Prince Henry's Men were granted livery for Prince Henry's funeral. Each man received four yards of cloth. The funeral was on 8 November 1612.
