Viewing Event Record: Patrick Brewe writes to Edward Alleyn about the sale of the Gill family property

Abstract

In a letter addressed to Edward Alleyn 'nere unto the pallace garden', Patrick Brewe reports on a dispute between members of the Gill family preventing the sale of inherited property. More documentation will be forthcoming, and he assures Alleyn that the sale is still viable.
