Viewing Event Record: Henry Wotton refers to the Cockpit as being in Shoe Lane

Abstract

In a letter of 3 June 1633 to Sir Edmund Bacon, Sir Henry Wotton refers to meeting Bacon's nephew Robert Bacon the 'other day at the Cock-pit in Shoe-lane (where my self am rara avis).'
