Viewing Event Record: Queen Anne's Men are licensed to perform at the Red Bull and Curtain

Abstract

A royal patent licences the Queen Anne's players to perform at their usual houses, the Red Bull and the Curtain, as well as in any other place within the realm. The patent names the actors of the company.
