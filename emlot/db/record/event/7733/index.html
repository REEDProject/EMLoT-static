Viewing Event Record: Commissioners of the Sewers assess Francis Langley's share of maintenance at Paris Garden

Abstract

The Surrey and Kent Commissioners for the Sewers assess Francis Langley's proportionate share of maintenance costs for the sluices and river walls connected to Paris Garden. Langley was rated for the acreage of the manor, 'containing in all 60 acres' and requiring 20s of contribution.
