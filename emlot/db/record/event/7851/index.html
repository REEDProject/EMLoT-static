Viewing Event Record: Henslowe records loans to Admiral's Men players and playwrights

Abstract

In his memorandum book, Philip Henslowe records loans to the Admiral's Men for various purposes, including the purchase of stage properties and payment advances for the composition of playbooks by dramatists such as Anthony Monday and Thomas Dekker.
