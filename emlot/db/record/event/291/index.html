Viewing Event Record: Philip Rosseter secures a patent to build a new playhouse in the Blackfriars precinct

Abstract

A patent secured by Philip Rosseter and his partners for building a new playhouse in the Blackfriars mentions the now-defunct Whitefriars where Rosseter had managed the company of the Children of the Revels.
