Viewing Event Record: Queen Anne's Men attend Count Arenberg at Durham Place

Abstract

Thomas Greene was paid Â£19 16s. on behalf of his fellow grooms of the chamber and Queen Anne's Men for attending and waiting upon Count Arenberg at Durham Place for eighteen days. The Count was a commissioner at the Somerset House Conference to negotiate a peace treaty between England and Spain.
