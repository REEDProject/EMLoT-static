Viewing Event Record: The tenants of the Fortune are in arrears of their rent

Abstract

The tenants of the Fortune are in arrears of their rent, in the sum of 132l 12s 6d; another quarter's rent, in the amount of 32l 1s 4d, is coming due at Michaelmas. If Dulwich College does not receive the rent in arrears in due time, it will be compelled 'to take moneys up at interest to supplie their wants, and relief of the poor of the said Colledge.'
