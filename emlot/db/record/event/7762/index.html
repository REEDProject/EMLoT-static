Viewing Event Record: Privy Council issues a travel license to the Lord Strange's Men

Abstract

The Privy Council issues a license to travel beyond London and perform plays to the Lord Strange's Men, permitting them to play 'in anie other Cities Townes & corporacions' sufficiently distant from London.
