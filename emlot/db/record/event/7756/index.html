Viewing Event Record: 'Gentlemen of the Court' and soldiers attend plays in London: Nashe

Abstract

Thomas Nashe reports that because plays occur during the 'idlest time of the day,' audience members usually comprise 'Gentlemen of the Court, the Innes of the Courte, and the number of Captaines and Souldiers' with time available for recreation.
