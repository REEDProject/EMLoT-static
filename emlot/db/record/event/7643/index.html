Viewing Event Record: Richard Cuckow is ordered to open a sluice at his gate, leading into the field next to the playhouse

Abstract

The Surrey and Kent Commissioners of the Sewers order Richard Cuckow to open a sluice 'or thorough' in his gate, leading into the field next to the playhouse.
