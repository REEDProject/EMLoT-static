Viewing Event Record: David Watson leases a garden plot adjacent to the Bear Garden to Thomas Burnaby

Abstract

David Watson leases a garden plot located between the Bear Garden and the common ditch to Thomas Burnaby, for a period of twenty one years, at a rent of six pounds biannually. The deed includes specific provision for repairs to the 'fence, pale and inclosures' that will prevent damage from the adjacent Bear Garden, including 'anie dogges, beares or otherwise.'
