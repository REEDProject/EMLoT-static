Viewing Event Record: A man is assaulted at the Fortune

Abstract

John Shawe of Grub Street, and Gilbert Borne of Whitecross Street, and Ralph Brewyn of St Clement Eastcheap, butchers, are bound for £20 and £40 respectively to appear before the Middlesex Justices of the Peace at the next sessions to answer 'for abusing certen gentlemen at the Play House called The Fortune.'
