Viewing Event Record: The Duke of Lennox and the Lord Chamberlain fight over a box at the Blackfriars: Garrard to Wentworth

Abstract

George Garrard writes to Lord Wentworth of an altercation between the Duke of Lennox and the Lord Chamberlain over a box at a new play at the Blackfriars.
