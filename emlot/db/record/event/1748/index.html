Viewing Event Record: Playing resumes following the plague

Abstract

An abstract from the Privy Council Register takes note of letters written to the Lord Mayor and justices of Middlesex and Surrey, ordering that the King's, Queen's and Prince's companies be allowed to play 'at their accustomed places.'
