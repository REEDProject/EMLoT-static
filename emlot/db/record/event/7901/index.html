Viewing Event Record: Elizabeth Condell makes her will

Abstract

Elizabeth Condell makes her will on 1 September 1635; the will is registered 18 February 1635/6. She leaves twenty shillings to her son, William Condell, to be taken 'out of the interest lease or Terme of yeares which I haue in the Globe, and ffryers,' with the additional provision that any additional amounts be dispensed to him as her executors 'thinke fitt,' and only in the case of 'his extreame poverty and need.' She leaves Elizabeth Wheaton 'the gatheringe Place at the Globe during my Lease,' as well as £20 and a variety of household goods.
