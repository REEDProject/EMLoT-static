Viewing Event Record: Requests, Burbage v Allen: Allen swears an affidavit

Abstract

Cuthbert Burbage sues Giles Allen in Requests court over the Theatre playhouse property. Giles Allen swears an affidavit 2 June 1600. Allen says that he does not have copies of the depositions made in this case, and lists other witnesses who are to depose on his behalf: Richard Parramore, Robert Vigerous, Thomas Nevill, Robert Miles, Ralph Miles, John Hyde, and William Gall.
