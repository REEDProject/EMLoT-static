Viewing Event Record: Stage plot describes performance structure of 'Frederick and Basilea' at the Rose playhouse

Abstract

A stage plot for the play 'Ffrederick & Basilea' describes the sequence of performance for the Admiral's Men company at the Rose playhouse, indicating roles as well as entrances and exits for the players.
