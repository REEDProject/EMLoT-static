Viewing Event Record: Apprentices illegally perform the 'Hog Hath Lost His Pearl' at the Whitefriars

Abstract

Sir Henry Wotton writes to Sir Edmund Bacon of a recent disturbance at the Whitefriars playhouse. Sixteen apprentices illegally performed a play entitled 'The Hog Hath Lost His Pearl' at the Whitefriars last Sunday to an audience of their mistresses. Toward the end of the play, the sheriffs broke into the playhouse and arrested six or seven of the culprits.
